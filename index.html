Wonder & Capture

Exploring the world's beauty through the lens of a traveler.

Welcome to Wander & Capture! This blog is where my love for travel and photography comes to life. Join me as I journey through some of the most breathtaking landscapes, from hidden forests to towering mountain ranges, capturing nature’s beauty in every shot.

About Me

Hi, I’m Ephy Kamau, a software engineer by profession and a passionate traveler, nature lover, and photographer at heart. When I’m not writing code, you’ll find me exploring new places, capturing the beauty of the natural world through my camera lens. This blog is my creative outlet, a space where I combine my love for traveling, nature, and photography to share my experiences and stories with you.

From the towering mountains and serene forests to the quiet moments in nature, I document what inspires me. Here, you’ll find glimpses of the world as I see it, along with tips, travel stories, and photography from my adventures.
